Johnny Be Good



Posted April 1st
by

Khalos

Streets after streets, always the same damn streets. I am the one that haunts the City. Roaming around like a zombie, from business tower to business tower, from junkyard to junkyard. Unknown, uncared about : the shadow of the City. The City that wants to kill me, trying to keep me close to her, breastfeeding on her sickness, trying to get me to take the pain away. I am fierce, a powerful, dangerous weapon to her. Turning all that is into myriads of me, turning her into a maze from inside, undiscovered. Dismantled, dismouted the City stands alone, a fort, but I cannot let her win this game. Gazing in alleys I see her reflection, looking for me, calling my name, trying to trick me into reuniting with her : my womb. Boulevards are nomansland, Avenues battlefields and the rivers that splits you in halfs is none other then the Styx.

Even you I can twist, reform, transform into my own. I am not one of your kids. I am increasingly thirsty of justice, but yours seems to have completly vanished. I have made enquiries about it, I have searched, and gathered all the hints I could but it just seemed you are empty of such a thing called justice. You are a matricial demon that feeds on her own disgusting descendance. A decadent City. But demons fade away when they die.

In the depths of her « râle » I can hear my voice too. I never really left her. We are two and we are one. We are foes and we are friends. I despise and love her. She is mine and yet I can't get a grasp on her. She's running through my veins, she's my mental landscape : the reflection of my own self. I'm floating through her, hand in hand with my death, dancing through the night. Antagonist at her I am all I reject and she's the One. The City that I hate, that I am, that I fight for and that I'm trying to destroy : myself.
